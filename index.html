Service program providing round-the-clock operational legal assistance to citizens of Russia and Georgia
The need in such 24-hour operational legal assistance is due to absence of diplomatic relations between Russia and Georgia. Hence, the result may be very likely stresses that occur in any country where you are staying, whose laws are unknown to you, and where conventional measures of support by diplomats from the Embassy of the Russian Federation are absent. The offered service of legal aid is based on protecting your interests by lawyers, attorneys, notaries public and representative of human rights advocacy organisations in Georgia who are fluent in Russian. They are professionals in a wide range of areas in national law and can promptly start solving your problems in the native language of airline companies, medicine, police, hotel managers, restaurant waitpersons, and representatives of local authorities and other official bodies.

When staying in Georgia, you can face issues demanding a knowledge of the specific features of not only the laws and respective bylaws, but also of the legal practice of their enforcement. For instance, in the event of a delayed arrival or departure of a flight, what is the obligation of the air carrier company? In case of an illegal, in your opinion, debiting of your account by a car rental company, what prompt steps should be taken? What is the course of your actions upon finding that your identity papers are missing? What are your rights to return of low-quality merchandise purchased in a shop when you are short of time? How to get in touch with Russian-speaking representatives of emergency services?

The service program includes the following main kinds and forms of protection of interests of Clients who have concluded a service agreement:

Urgent consultancy: oral prompt consultancy on all legal issues concerning the Client personally is conducted within two hours immediately upon of contacting the call center;

Urgent consultancy: oral 24-hour consultancy on all urgent matters occurring in the country of staying is provided depending on operative situation;

Prompt connection with a lawyer by the Client's telephone to talk with opponent in the country of staying;

Arrival of a lawyer in the Client's country of staying to the scene of action in the event of apprehension of the Client or his/her bringing to responsibility by representatives of official bodies. The lawyer shall announce the time of arrival during the urgent call from the Client's phone;

Preparing litigation documents according to the requirements of the legislation of the Client's country of staying on matters related personally to him or her. Documents related to a Client's business are prepared on a Client's separate request on terms and conditions additionally agreed with the Client;

Calling emergency services by request of the Client for urgent response in the territory of the country of staying;

Call-back: Relieving the Client from telephone connection payment in the country of staying by providing the client with an incoming call;

24-hour providing by Client's requests of information on the options of various services in the country of staying, including booking travel, accommodation мhotels, apartments or villas; car rental services, transfers, insurance advice, currency exchange advice, information about restaurants, and purchasing tickets for sport and cultural events in Georgia.

Services are provided by the Georgian organisation Centre for International Projects and professionals of the St. Petersburg United Bar Association acting based on a bilateral agreement between the mentioned legal entities.
